Interpolation (cmlabs.interpolate)

Sub-package for interpolation methods.

cmlabs.interpolate.lagrange(x_arr, y_arr, x)[source]

Lagrange interpolation polynomial value at x.

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather} P_n(x) = L_n(x) = \sum_{i=0}^{n} l_i(x) f(x_i) \\ l_i(x) = \prod_{j=0, j \neq i}^{n} \frac{x - x_j}{x_i - x_j} \end{gather}\end{split}\]

where \(l_i(x)\) is the Lagrange basis polynomial for the i-th data point.

Parameters

x_arrnumpy.ndarray

The x-coordinates of the data points.

y_arrnumpy.ndarray

The y-coordinates of the data points.

xnumpy.float32

The x-coordinate at which to evaluate the polynomial.

Returns

lagrange: numpy.float32

The value of the polynomial at x.
